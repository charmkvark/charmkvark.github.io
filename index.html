Information about the game

[Game name] consists of smaller mini games where you play it and compete against your oppenent. The game is played on the same machine and keyboard.

Each mini game won will move you one step closer to the opponents home base - your target. When you reach the target, you win. The first minigame is a fighting game, the second is a fps and in the last game, you will have to avoid walls to survive.
