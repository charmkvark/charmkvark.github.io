About the Game

Frozen Combat is a local two player PC game, consisting of several mini games where you compete and play against your opponent. The game is played on the same machine and keyboard.

Each mini game won will move you one step closer to your home flag. When you reach the flag, you win.

Brawl

Face each other in a nerve-wracking 2D fist-to-fist combat. Use attack moves such as Karate Chop and Kung Fu Kick to give your opponent real damage. Duck, jump and block to avoid the other players attacks.


Take advantage of the platforms to gain high ground, and combine moves such as duck-and-kick or jump-and-chop for a cool fighting style. The last snowman standing wins.

Snowball Fight


Engage in a thrilling 3D snowball fight. Collect ammunition from respawning snow piles, and use crates and platforms as cover. Control the throwing-distance of the snowballs, as you launch them against the other player.


If your inventory is low, use the more risky option and self inflict damage as you pick snowballs from yourself. Regain this lost health later in snow piles. The last snowman standing wins.

Skiing


Put your skills to the test in the dangerous slopes of alpine skiing. Accelerate or slow down your speed for better maneuverability. Use moves such as jump or duck to dodge incoming obstacles such as trees, barrels and crates.


When the cooldown is full, use the shove-action to push your adversary into an obstacle (this is no gentleman sport). The last snowman standing wins.

Ready to play?

What are you waiting for? Download the game and challenge your friend in the most epic frozen combat game ever.


One more thing!

As this game is played locally on one computer, we know that the pandemic can prevent you from enjoying this with your friends. However, there is a life hack that still allows you to play online and compete against one another. For this, it requires that both clients have Zoom installed. Detailed instructions on how to set up your environment can be followed in the README.md file that is included when you download the game.
